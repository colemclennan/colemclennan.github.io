Cole McLennan

My name is Cole McLennan, I am an award winning photographer who has never shot a wedding before. I am currently studying at Algonquin College and plan to apply the techniques I have learned at your wedding. If this sounds like something you're interested in please reach out using the contact form at the bottom of the page.

I have been doing photography for over two years and have plenty of experience in different photography applications. Wedding photography is more than just taking photos. It's my belief that wedding photographers are there to help guide you along your day and ensure that it goes on without a hitch. If you were to hire me as your photographer, you wouldn't have to worry about the documentation of your day. Your mind can rest, as I can assure you I will beautifully capture the most special day of your lives.
