Cole McLennan

My name is Cole McLennan, I am an award winning photographer who has never shot a wedding before. I am currently studying at Algonquin College and plan to apply the techniques I have learned at your wedding. If this sounds like something you're interested in please reach out using the contact form at the bottom of the page.

I have been doing photography for over two years and have plenty of experience in different photography applications. Wedding photography is more than just taking photos. It's my belief that wedding photographers are there to help guide you along your day and ensure that it goes on without a hitch. If you were to hire me as your photographer, you wouldn't have to worry about the documentation of your day. Your mind can rest, as I can assure you I will beautifully capture the most special day of your lives.

Packages Include

Engagement Shoot

Spend a few hours with me in your favourite part of town. We'll roam around and take pictures in a variety of areas to ensure that there are shots that you'll love. My goal with this shoot is to get you excited about your wedding day, and confident that I'll deliver shots you'll love when that day comes.

Full Day Coverage

From start to finish, I will be there to document your special day. Packages include up to 8 hours of coverage. This means that everything from getting ready, to the reception will be covered. If you want to ensure that nothing is missed we can discuss pricing for a second photographer to attend.

Hundreds of Photos

It's my job to make sure that there isn't a moment missed that you'd like to remember. I will deliver hundreds of photos taken throughout the day, with a large focus on the main events during the day. I believe it's important to showcase all the wonderful people who are attending your wedding as well, and will do my best to deliver shots of as many guests as I can.
